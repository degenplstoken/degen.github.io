Vision

Degen is a memetoken created with the aim of growing a successful financial ecosystem for everyone. All successful cryptocurrency have a sustainable economy based on strong fundamentals of utility, scarcity and community power. Degen is deflationary, with a stablecoin economy and strong community which will ensure its sustainability.
